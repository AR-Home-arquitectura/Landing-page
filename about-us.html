About The Team

At AR Home, we believe that home decoration is more than just filling a space with furniture. It's an expression of individuality and creativity. Our platform revolutionizes the way people design and personalize their homes by introducing augmented reality into the process. We strive to provide our users with the ability to visualize and experiment with a wide variety of furniture and decor items in their own spaces before making a purchase. This not only drives innovation in decoration but also reduces resource waste by enabling more informed buying decisions. Our team is committed to delivering an exceptional user experience and staying at the forefront of the latest design trends. We are constantly evolving to ensure that AR Home remains the top choice for those looking to create homes that reflect their unique style. Join us in this exciting revolution in home decoration. With AR Home, your home is your canvas, and technology is your brush. Let's transform your ideas into sustainable and creative reality. Welcome to AR Home!
